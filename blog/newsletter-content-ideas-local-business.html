50 Newsletter Content Ideas for Local Businesses

June 14, 2026 • 6 min read

Staring at a blank page wondering what to write for this week's newsletter? You're not alone. Coming up with fresh content week after week is the hardest part of email marketing.

Here are 50 content ideas organized by business type. Steal them, adapt them, and never run out of things to say.

For Restaurants

  1. New menu item announcement with photo
  2. Chef spotlight — who's behind the food
  3. Behind-the-scenes prep photos
  4. Customer review of the week
  5. Seasonal special preview
  6. Event night promotion (trivia, live music)
  7. Holiday catering menu
  8. Wine or drink pairing recommendations
  9. Local farm/ingredient sourcing story
  10. "Where are they now?" staff update

For Salons & Spas

  1. Before/after transformation photos
  2. Seasonal hair color trends
  3. Staff introduction series
  4. Product recommendation of the month
  5. Booking availability — last-minute openings
  6. Holiday look inspiration
  7. Skin care tips for the season
  8. Client appreciation spotlight
  9. New service introduction
  10. Gift card promotion

For Gyms & Fitness Studios

  1. Member success story / transformation
  2. Weekly workout tip with video
  3. Class schedule highlights
  4. Nutrition advice from a trainer
  5. Referral challenge announcement
  6. New equipment or class intro
  7. Upcoming event or competition
  8. Stretch of the week
  9. Recipe of the week
  10. Motivational quote + member photo

For Home Services (Plumbers, Electricians, HVAC)

  1. Seasonal maintenance checklist
  2. Emergency preparedness tips
  3. Before/after project photos
  4. Customer testimonial spotlight
  5. How-to guide (e.g., "5 signs your water heater needs replacing")
  6. Special offer — first-time customer discount
  7. Employee spotlight — introduce your team
  8. Common mistake to avoid
  9. New service announcement
  10. Energy-saving tips for the season

For Dentists & Vets

  1. New patient / new pet special offer
  2. Seasonal health tips
  3. Staff introduction series
  4. Myth vs. fact (common misconception)
  5. Patient/pet of the month
  6. New technology or equipment intro
  7. Holiday hours announcement
  8. Financing or payment plan options
  9. Before/after smile makeover
  10. FAQ — most common questions answered

The 50/30/20 Rule

For best results, use this content mix: 50% value (tips, education, entertainment), 30% promotional (offers, new services), 20% brand (staff stories, behind-the-scenes). This keeps subscribers engaged and prevents unsubscribes.
